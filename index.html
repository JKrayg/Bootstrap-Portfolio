About

Hello! My name is Jake Krayger. I am a 23 year old student from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. A little bit about me, I grew up just outside of Northeast Philadelphia in a small neighborhood called Rockledge with my Mother, my two brother's and our two jack russel's. I played sports for the local Philadlephia rec center just inside of Northeast Philadelphia called Fox Chase. There I played soccer, baseball and basketball. Around age 14 I left baseball and basketball behind but continued to play soccer for another 6 years into high school.

I graduated from Abington Sr High School in 2014 and immediately started working because I was in no rush to be in debt from college. I worked at a financial company called Finance of America as an assistant facilities manager (fancy for janitor). For as long as I can remember I've been fascinated by computers, I remember when I was around 13 years old I would mod the Call of Duty titles of that time on the xbox 360 (I thought I was so cool). Now I am a part of UPENN's coding bootcamp and I couldn't be happier and excited for my future. I saved money and years of debt. I will also have a career that is secure in the future because I dont think computers are going anywhere anytime soon.
